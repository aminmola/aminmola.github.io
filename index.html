    Prof. M. Babazade

    Real-Time Pricing for Smart Grid with Distributed Energy

    With the increasing integration of distributed energy (DE) and storage devices in the power market, energy provision has become more complex. My research focused on formulating a real-time pricing (RTP) model for the smart grid, which balances real-time demand and shifts peak-hour load. By modeling RTP as a noncooperative game, I developed an online distributed algorithm to optimize energy consumption, generation, and storage strategies, ensuring privacy for both users and providers. The approach demonstrated effective peak load reduction and supply-demand balancing in numerical simulations.
